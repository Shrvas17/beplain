About Us

In 1904, beplain was founded by Alfred Amour Garnier in Blois, Paris. Starting from hair care products with natural ingredients, beplain continues to grow and begin to wrestle the world of skin care. beplain believes that nature has provided the best self-care solutions, so that beplain products always use natural ingredients.

Not only about beauty, Garnier is also committed to minimizing the impact on the environment as much as possible. Garnier has always developed packaging and product formulas that can be biodegradable.Now beplain has grown into an international body care brand with 7 main product categories. beplain has a range of treatments that can meet the various needs of consumers. For beplain, everyone deserves the best body care at an affordable price.
